CSS: Color

Why Color is so Important

When it comes to web design color is what can make or break it. Although format is important know one will notice if you can't even read the text. We must learn color theory in order to make sure the the website is easy to read and great to look at.

Text and Background Coloring

Changing the background color on website is very entertaining and you can come up with some very creative things. However, this is no good unless you can read the content on your page. When it comes to font and background contrast it is suggested that for regular text a contrast ratio of 4.5 : 1 in order to prevent this. A ratio of 3 : 1 is suggested when dealing with large text. These ratios have been tested to that even those with color deficiencies and moderate low vision may be able to read the text.
